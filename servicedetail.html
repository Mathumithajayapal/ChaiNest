🍵 Perfect Brew Guide

1

Heat Fresh Water

Bring filtered water to 95°C (just below boiling). Fresh water ensures pure flavor.

2

Measure Your Tea

Add 1 teaspoon of Masala Chai Gold per cup (250ml) to a teapot or infuser.

3

Steep with Patience

Pour water over leaves and steep for 3-5 minutes. Longer steep = stronger spice notes.

4

Add Milk (Optional)

For traditional chai, add warm milk and sweeten with honey or jaggery to taste.

5

Sip Mindfully

Pour, pause, and savor the aroma before each sip. Let it warm your soul.
