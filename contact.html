Frequently asked questions

━━ quick answers to common questions ━━
Do you ship internationally? ▼
Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping typically takes 5-10 business days. Free shipping on orders over $75. We use eco-friendly, compostable packaging for all international orders to minimize our carbon footprint.
Can I visit for a tea tasting? ▼
Absolutely! Our flagship store in Pune offers daily guided tastings from 11 AM to 6 PM. Walk-ins are welcome, or you can book a private chai ceremony for groups of 4+ people. Contact us to reserve your spot and experience our curated blends firsthand.
Do you offer wholesale pricing? ▼
Yes, we partner with cafes, hotels, retailers, and restaurants worldwide. Email wholesale@chainest.com with your business details, estimated monthly volume, and location. We offer tiered pricing, custom blends, and white-label options for qualified partners.
